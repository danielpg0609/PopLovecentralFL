Welcome to PopLove Gelato Pops

PopLove Gelato Pops represent the perfect fusion of tropical fruits, classic flavors, and the exquisite artistry of Italian gelato. We are passionate about crafting artisanal gelato on a stick that brings joy to your taste buds. Our commitment to excellence drives us to create premium, low-sugar popsicles. Through extensive journeys to Italy and countless hours of research, we've mastered our recipes to bring you a truly unique and delightful product. POPSICLE FLAVORS: WATER BASED Our fruit-based popsicles are a guilt-free indulgence, with only 86 calories each. Made from 100% natural fruit, they contain no preservatives, additives, or added sugars. What you'll find, though, is an abundance of flavor that awakens your senses. Our journey began with a simple mission: to offer you a healthier, yet delicious frozen treat. Experience the freshness of tropical fruits in every bite, as we blend tradition and innovation in every PopLove Gelato Pop.
